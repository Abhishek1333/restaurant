ABOUT OUR RESTAURANT

Journey of Delicious Food, through the ages

It all started more than 75 years ago. A list of secret herbs and spices scratched out on the kitchen door, that continues to be followed across 145 countries, with more than 800,000 team members breading and freshly preparing bucket after bucket of our signature Hot & Crispy chicken. The Colonel’s Secret Recipe continues to be as guarded; and our commitment towards Food, People and Planet remains as steadfast. With the new spicy table opening every 6 hours, we power on to serve the World’s Tastiest Chicken!
